Projects

Echo Rush

Echo Rush is a single-player 2D platformer developed during my Game Design Methodologies class over the course of three months for an assignment. It was created using the Unity Engine and follows a baby bat as it journeys through caves, mountains, and forests in search of its family.

Primal Punch Up

Primal Punch Up is a 2-4 player local multiplayer fighting game developed during Game Design Studio 1 using the Unity Engine. Players choose an animal ninja to play as and use their unique abilities to defeat their friends and get as many points as possible.

Caved In

Caved In is a 4-8 player online game developed in Unreal Engine in Game Design Studio 2. Players must navigate a large cave system to collect enough plant samples to open the elevator to return to the surface. Injured players become 'infected' and must sabotage the others without being caught.
