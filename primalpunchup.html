About the Project

Primal Punch Up is a 2-4 player game where you and your friends choose a hero from a selection of animal ninjas and battle in a local PVP experience. Become victorious by collecting the most fruit and protecting them until the time runs out. Discover unique powerups and traps and use them to defeat your enemies and steal their fruit. Choose your strategy to ensure you are victorious and become the ultimate champion!

It was created over a period of three months using the Unity Game Engine in Game Design Studio 1, a third year subject in the Bachelor of Game Development.

My Contribution

Along with another student, I helped to develop several main mechanics of the game, created all the unique items, designed all the maps, and designed the UI.

What I Have Learned

Through development I learnt how to utilise the Agile development model to create a game in a team. Through 4 Sprints over the course of the subject, we held regular SCRUM meetings and playtests to recieve feedback on our game, which we then analysed to better improve for the next sprint. I learnt a lot about the Unity Engine and used several features I hadn't had experience with before, such as UI, 3D animations and 3D modelling.
