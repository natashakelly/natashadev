About the Event

Our team was given the opportunity to showcase Primal Punch at the Student Games Showcase at South by SouthWest Sydney 2024.

What I Learned

South by SouthWest 2024 was a great opportunity to meet other student developers and play their games, as well as recieve feedback on ours. Our team attended many guest lectures as part of the SXSW Early Career program, where we learnt much about the current game industry and how to start our careers as game developers. We were also able to attend the Games Showcase and view many amazing Australian and International games and talk with up and coming developers.
