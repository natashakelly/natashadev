About the Project

Echo Rush is a 2D platformer where you play as a young bat who has been accidentally left behind by his family. Echolocate to "see" through the darkness, maneuver through obstacles, and chase after your family!

It was created over a period of three months using the Unity Game Engine in the Game Design Methodologies class, a second year subject in the Bachelor of Game Development.

My Contribution

Along with two other students, I helped to develop several main mechanics of the game, as well as creating Level 1.

What I Have Learned

The development of this game taught me much about project management in the game development industry through the use of the Agile development model, including multiple Sprints and regular SCRUM meetings. Regular playtesting allowed me to learn how to give and recieve meaningful feedback and make improvements to the game based on it. I also learnt how to use the Unity Game Engine and Github to code alongside a team, utilising the C# coding language.
